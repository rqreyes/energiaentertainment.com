Exciting. Fun. Memorable.

Based in San Diego, CA, Energia Entertainment produces exciting and unique live shows. We bring the most talented artists in the city to make your event unforgettable. Whether you are planning a small, intimate gathering or a big spectacular celebration, we devised various packages to get your party started right. We entertain at birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, festivals, parades, and private parties.

Energia is the only team in San Diego to create Ladies Nights: All Male Burlesque Showcases - perfect for bachelorette parties and Girls' Nights Out!

Mixing in the Visual Arts by exhibiting painting and photography, streaming film, and performing Live Body Art.

Specializing in Brazilian samba we flaunt colorful, sexy dancers to bring the Spirit of Carnaval to your occasion.

Showcasing dancers versed in ballroom, contemporary, jazz, hip hop, capoeira, cirque, and other genres.

Catering to your ultimate event experience with profressional live bands, drummers, DJs, and singers.
